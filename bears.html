Polar Bear (Ursus maritimus) Maternal Den Locations in the Beaufort and Chukchi Seas and Nearby Areas, 1910-2018

An R Notebook by Brandi E. Markert

Examining polar bear den locations. Using data downloaded from United States Geological Survey.

Data source: Durner, G. M., 2010, Catalogue of Polar Bear (Ursus maritimus) maternal den locations in the Beaufort and Chukchi seas and nearby areas, 1910-2018 (ver. 2.0, January 2020): U.S. Geological Survey data release, https://doi.org/10.5066/P9RPHH50.
